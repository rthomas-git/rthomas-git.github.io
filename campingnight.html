Camping Night

Camping Night is a third person casual simulation game where you can experience the great adventures of the outdoors. You'll be able to discover new people who's on the same journey as you and gain their friendship by doing specific tasks for them all throughout the woods, but make sure you keep a close eye on your campfire!

Project Details:

Platform: Window PC | Engine & Language: UE4 Blueprints | Duration: 2.5 Weeks | Size: 4

Role Overview:

  • Orchestrated the comprehensive development of main player mechanics, ensuring a robust and responsive gameplay experience from conception to execution.
  • Formed a tight-knit collaboration with the designer to seamlessly integrate the Heads-Up Display (HUD), enhancing the player's interface and overall gaming immersion.
  • Collaborated with the design team to meticulously refine and implement various UI/UX elements, contributing to a polished and user-friendly visual design.
  • Played a vital role as a Quality Assurance (QA) resource, actively testing builds and player mechanics, providing valuable insights to fine-tune and optimize the gaming experience.
