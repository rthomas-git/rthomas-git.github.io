Hazel: Tap
Away Climate Change

Come join Ralph and his friends as they combat climate change in their portion of the metaverse, the Hazelverse! Swipe away carbon, turn captured carbon into diamonds, earn upgrades and unlock Critters. You can also unlock your personal Hazel island, which will be a unique, only-one-of-its kind. Best of all, you can directly support carbon removal efforts in the real world through Hazel -- and when you do, you'll earn valuable rewards that enhance your gameplay!
