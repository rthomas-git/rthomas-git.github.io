Fly South

Fly South, a top down shooter minigame where you play as a young lost blue jay (Jay) that has to migrate south for the winter, but along the way you will encounter flocks of crows and Falcons that tries to stop you.

Project Details:

Platform: Windows PC | Engine & Language: Unity C# | Duration: 2 Weeks | Size: 4

Role Overview:

  • Developed core game mechanics from conceptualization to execution, ensuring a seamless and engaging player experience.
  • Engineered sophisticated enemy AI algorithms for both levels, enhancing the game's challenge and strategic depth.
  • Orchestrated the seamless integration of various level mechanics, ensuring a cohesive and immersive gameplay environment.
  • Crafted and implemented an intuitive main game Heads-Up Display (HUD), providing players with essential information and enhancing overall user interface design.
  • Designed and programmed an efficient game progression and save system, allowing players to seamlessly navigate through the gaming experience while ensuring their progress is securely recorded and retrievable.
