Abigor

Abigor is a third person action adventure game taking place in the Alastor Demon Hunting Academy over a three day period, where you will learn how to use various weapons, cast magical spells, and fight a variety of demons. Each battle and encounter will equip you with the needed skills and tools for the epic final battle.

Project Details:

Platform: Windows PC | Engine & Language: UE4 Blueprints | Duration: 10 Months | Size: 15

Role Overview:

  • Managed a team and directed project tasks, focusing on programming as a lead.
  • Developed 9 diverse enemy AI using behavior trees and scripting, in collaboration with combat designers (grunts, mini-bosses, and level bosses).
  • Enhanced gameplay experience by closely working with combat designers on AI behavior and combat mechanics.
  • Contributed as a QA to test builds, player mechanics, combat mechanics, and AI.
